FAQ

Do you offer same-day service?

Do you take appliances and furniture?

Yes—appliances, mattresses, couches, office furniture, and more. We donate when possible.

Do you do light demolition?

Yes—sheds, decks, hot tubs, playsets, plus carpet/tile removal. We haul debris and leave a sweep-clean finish.

What payment methods do you take?

Cash, check, Venmo, and Cash App.
